THE WORSHIP

Our Sunday Services starts at 10:00 am-12pm. It is a warm and welcoming gathering that caters and accommodates youth, new, young to mature Christians.

We teach and preach the word of God that all may be feed and brought to full maturity in Christ Jesus as the Scripture says “Desire the sincere word of God that we may not be thrown from and to by winds of doctrine’’ so that mediocrity and crisis in the Christendom can be brought under subjection while increasing the level of spiritual growth

Our services are modern Pentecostal style with songs that ranges from modern contemporary to classical Gospel songs to suit everyone ones need. We give room for the flow of the power of the God to move in prayer and ministration to work among us while fellowshipping.
