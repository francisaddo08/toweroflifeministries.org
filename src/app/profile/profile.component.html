PASTOR PATRICK OBI
Profile & History

PROFILE

He is better known for his biblical and practical approach to deliverance Ministrations in Nigeria and London which has seen numerous numbers of afflicted lives and tougher cases restored.
He has been nicknamed the Walking Bible by his flocks, because ‘’He has an insight of the revelation to every part of the bible you are not acquainted with.”
He will bring it to full understanding with scriptural backup truth, whether he is in the church or outside he has the biblical chapters and verses clearly in his head.
You will be left not in doubt, for you will receive the knowledge and revelation together according to the gospel truth. He is a true Shepherd who encourages his flocks in the way he articulates himself as he imparts the word to either an individual or a group.
Anointed by God, Pastor Obi Patrick is a True Prophet of God, Power House Evangelist, a great Teacher and Preacher of the true Word of God.
Many people who have not been able to live their God ordained lives before are daily receiving their freedom through his Ministry.
He has a thriving and prosperous congregation and he is putting a mark in London that will lift God’s people to live and reign in dominion according to the fullness of God’s power.

PERSONAL HISTORY

Pastor Obi was called into the ministry with the mandate to set captives free and to demonstrate the life and power of God in the world.
He was born into a Christian family but has never been satisfied with their standard of Christianity.
At a tender age, he was desperate to know God more and to have a deeper relationship with Him.
In his search for true and deeper relationship with God, Jesus appeared to him one day as he was praying and filled him with the Holy Spirit.
Since then like wide fire he spread the gospel in and across the country.
After his ordination as a pastor, God gave him the mandate to set the captives free and to demonstrate His life and power in the world. He was wondering what God meant.
He said to him” I will show you”. That night, God showed him in a dream where demons blocked the road and held many hostage. He came and subdued them and set the people free.
He told him to start a ministry and gave him the name “Tower of Life” and promised to back him.
He started the churches in Nigeria and London and captives are being set free in these churches with an uncommon anointing of the Holy Spirit.
